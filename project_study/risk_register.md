# AEAMC Project - Risk Register

This document identifies and tracks potential risks to the AEAMC project. Each risk is assessed for its likelihood and impact, and a mitigation strategy is proposed.

| Risk ID | Risk Description                                                                | Category             | Likelihood (1-5) | Impact (1-5) | Risk Score | Mitigation Strategy                                                                                             | Owner          |
| :------ | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | :------------------- | :--------------- | :----------- | :--------- | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :------------- |
| T-001   | A vulnerability in the `MicroblockAnchor.sol` smart contract could be exploited. | Technical            | 3 (Medium)       | 5 (High)     | 15         | - Conduct a full third-party security audit of the smart contract. <br>- Implement a bug bounty program.        | Engineering    |
| T-002   | The centralized coordinator becomes a single point of failure or bottleneck.      | Technical            | 4 (High)         | 4 (High)     | 16         | - Design and implement a decentralized coordinator selection mechanism. <br>- Load test the coordinator.          | Engineering    |
| T-003   | Mobile device heterogeneity leads to SDK compatibility and performance issues.  | Technical            | 4 (High)         | 3 (Medium)   | 12         | - Implement a comprehensive device testing strategy. <br>- Use a feature flagging system to roll out new features. | Engineering    |
| T-004   | Network latency and reliability impact the user experience and task success rate. | Technical            | 3 (Medium)       | 3 (Medium)   | 9          | - Implement a more robust error handling and retry mechanism in the SDK. <br>- Optimize the network protocol.   | Engineering    |
| L-001   | The network's reward token is classified as an unregistered security.           | Legal & Compliance   | 3 (Medium)       | 5 (High)     | 15         | - Obtain a formal legal opinion from a law firm specializing in cryptocurrency. <br>- Design the tokenomics to emphasize utility. | Legal          |
| L-002   | The collection of device telemetry violates data privacy laws (GDPR/CCPA).      | Legal & Compliance   | 4 (High)         | 4 (High)     | 16         | - Implement a comprehensive privacy policy and user consent mechanism. <br>- Anonymize telemetry data where possible. | Legal & Eng.   |
| L-003   | The project's patent application is not granted or is challenged.               | Legal & Compliance   | 2 (Low)          | 3 (Medium)   | 6          | - Work with a patent lawyer to draft a strong application. <br>- Conduct a thorough prior art search.            | Legal          |
| O-001   | Failure to attract a sufficient number of mobile devices to the network.        | Operational          | 4 (High)         | 5 (High)     | 20         | - Develop a clear incentive model for participation. <br>- Launch a marketing campaign targeting mobile users.   | Product & Mktg |
| O-002   | A poor user experience in the mobile app deters participation.                  | Operational          | 3 (Medium)       | 4 (High)     | 12         | - Conduct user research and usability testing. <br>- Continuously iterate on the app's design and functionality. | Product & UX   |
| M-001   | Competitors with more established platforms capture the target market.          | Market               | 3 (Medium)       | 4 (High)     | 12         | - Clearly differentiate the project based on energy efficiency and mobile-first design. <br>- Foster a strong developer community. | Product        |
